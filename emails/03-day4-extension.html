The portal shows you the campaigns. The Chrome extension accepts them automatically, while you sleep.
 
Here's how it works:
 
1. Install the Creator Coders Chrome extension
2. Go to Amazon Creator Connections and click Capture Amazon Session in the popup
3. Set your auto-accept rules in Settings (min commission %, categories, brand filters)
4. Turn on automation
 
Every 2 hours, during your active window (default 8am-8pm), it scans your queue and accepts campaigns that match your rules. You don't have to touch anything.
