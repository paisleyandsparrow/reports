# Creator Coders Social Media Promotion Plan

## Product Overview
**Creator Coders** is automation software for creators/streamers to:
- Discover high-performing affiliate campaigns across Creator Connections, Associates, Meta
- Filter out noise → focus only on campaigns worth their time
- Auto-track ad ROI and accept campaigns automatically
- **No spreadsheets. No copy-paste. Just income.**

**Core Messaging:**
- "Software for serious creators"
- Automates what used to take hours (doing it manually)
- Built by creators who did it the hard way
- $100/month, 7 days free

**Post Ideas:**
